## Description
With error_user, the "Add to cart" buttons for items 3, 4 and 6 
do not respond, preventing them from being added to the cart both 
from the inventory page and from each product's individual page.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Log in with error_user / secret_sauce
2. On the inventory page, attempt to click "Add to cart" 
   on each of the 6 products
3. Observe which ones respond and which ones do not

## Expected Result
All products should be correctly added to the cart.

## Actual Result
Only items 1, 2 and 5 are added to the cart. Items 3, 4 and 6 
do not respond to the "Add to cart" click.

## Impact
The user cannot purchase 50% of the available catalog, 
resulting in direct loss of sales.
