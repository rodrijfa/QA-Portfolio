## Description
When authenticating with `visual_user`, inventory products display 
incorrect images that do not correspond to the listed items.
Unrelated images are shown, including a photo of a raccoon.

## Steps to Reproduce
1. Navigate to https://www.saucedemo.com
2. Log in with username: `visual_user` / password: `secret_sauce`
3. Observe the product images on the inventory page

## Expected Result
Successful login. Product images in inventory are correct and 
correspond to each item.

## Actual Result
"Sauce Labs Backpack" item displays an incorrect image.

## Impact
A real user would not be able to visually identify products before 
purchasing, directly affecting the shopping experience and potentially 
leading to returns or loss of trust in the platform.
